Technical Support & Project Supply Services

From initial engineering alignment to final site delivery

We provide comprehensive technical and commercial support for complex industrial projects. We mitigate risks, verify compliance, and streamline the procurement lifecycle for EPC contractors and industrial end-users.

Our services

Project Sourcing

We identify and qualify global manufacturers that match your exact project requirements, focusing on approved manufacturer lists (AML/VNDL), high-tier technical capability, and commercial competitiveness.

Technical Clarification

Our engineering team reviews data sheets, drawings, and specifications to align technical requirements, resolve technical queries (TQs), and eliminate discrepancies before manufacturing begins.

Supplier Coordination

We provide dedicated desk and field expediting, monitoring production milestones directly at manufacturing facilities to ensure strict adherence to project schedules and delivery timelines.

QA/QC Inspection Support

We coordinate comprehensive quality control activities, verify Material Test Reports (MTRs), and facilitate Third-Party Inspections (TPI) with agencies like SGS, TÜV, and Intertek to guarantee full asset compliance.

Logistics Coordination

We manage end-to-end supply chain logistics, including complex, out-of-gauge (OOG) industrial cargo, ensuring customs clearance and safe, on-time delivery directly to your project site.

Documentation Support

We compile, review, and manage critical project documentation, delivering fully compliant Manufacturing Data Books (MDB), sectional drawings, and material certificates required by industrial end-users.

EPC Package Support

We provide turnkey procurement management for complex EPC packages, consolidating multiple product categories into a single, fully coordinated technical and commercial stream.
